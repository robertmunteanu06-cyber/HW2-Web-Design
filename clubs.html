Why join a club?

Joining a club is the best way to make friends on campus. You can learn new skills, build your resume, and get out of your dorm room!

How to start your own club

If you don't see a club you like, you can start one! All you need is:

  1. An idea for the club.
  2. 5 friends to join.
  3. A teacher to supervise you.

Go to the Sign Up page to message us if you want to create a new club!
